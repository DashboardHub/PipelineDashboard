Welcome to DashboardHub! To get you started...

Welcome to DashboardHub, the no-setup, open-source platform that makes your deployment pipeline more transparent by ensuring everybody on your team is on the same page. DashboardHub does this by gathering the most relevant, up-to-date information (like what version is on what stage, build/deployment durations) all in one place, and monitors your deployments every 30 minutes and after each deployment to ensure everything is still up and running.

For more detailed help & FAQs.
